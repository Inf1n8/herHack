ABOUT US

Women Techmakers is an initiative by Google aiming to provide visibility and resources, and initiate a community for women in technology. As part of this global network, WTM VIT Vellore is on a mission to push women out of their comfort zone and ease them into the technical sphere.

HER HACK

Women Techmakers Vellore is proudly set to organize “HerHack”, a premium event in VIT University crafted along with the expansive GDG VIT community. The event is scheduled to take place on March 11, 2018.

We are conducting milestone technical workshops leading up to the 12 hour hackathon, focusing on introducing the participants to different domains of technology namely Web Development, Android and UI/UX.
