ABOUT US

GDG VIT is proud to be a part of the largest developer community in the world. Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are local groups of developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the Maps API, YouTube API and Google Calendar API. At GDG VIT, our team is committed to excellence and we believe in pushing ourselves to learn and to improve as well as help the local community, be it completing projects at GSoC, organizing DevFests, hosting workshops or contributing to open source projects throughout the year.

WOMEN TECHIES

Google Developers Group VIT is proudly set to organize “Women Techies”, a premium event in VIT University crafted along with the expansive GDG VIT community. Like all GDG VIT events, this event is free and is scheduled to take place on March 11, 2018.

We have conducted milestone workshops leading up to the 12 hour hackathon, focusing on introducing the participants to different domains of technology namely Web Development, Android and UI/UX.
